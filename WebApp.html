Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our web application development service

How long does it take to develop a web application?
The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. Simple applications take 4-6 weeks, medium complexity 8-12 weeks, and complex enterprise solutions 12-24 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?
Yes, we offer various support packages including bug fixes, security updates, feature enhancements, and performance optimization. Our standard projects include 30-90 days of free support depending on the package.
Can you work with our existing development team?
Absolutely! We frequently collaborate with in-house teams, providing specialized expertise, additional resources, or guidance on best practices and architecture. We adapt to your team's workflow and tools.
What is your approach to project management?
We use agile methodology with regular sprints, daily stand-ups, and weekly demos. You'll have full visibility into the development process through our project management tools and regular progress reports.
How do you ensure the security of web applications?
Security is integrated throughout our development process. We follow OWASP guidelines, conduct security audits, implement proper authentication/authorization, and perform penetration testing before deployment.
What happens after the project is completed?
We provide comprehensive documentation, conduct training sessions for your team, and hand over all source code and assets. We also offer ongoing support packages to ensure your application continues to perform optimally.
