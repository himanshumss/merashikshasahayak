Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start a new project?
We can typically start new projects within 1-2 weeks after initial discussion and requirements gathering. For urgent projects, we offer expedited onboarding.
Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?
Yes, we offer various support packages including bug fixes, updates, and maintenance. Our standard projects include 30-90 days of free support depending on the package.
What industries do you specialize in?
We have experience across multiple industries including healthcare, finance, education, e-commerce, logistics, and manufacturing. Our solutions are tailored to your specific industry needs.
Can you work with our existing development team?
Absolutely! We frequently collaborate with in-house teams, providing specialized expertise, additional resources, or guidance on best practices and architecture.
What is your typical project timeline?
Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites take 4-6 weeks, medium applications 8-12 weeks, and complex enterprise solutions 12-24 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during proposal.
Do you offer training for the solutions you develop?
Yes, we provide comprehensive training sessions, documentation, and ongoing support to ensure your team can effectively use and maintain the solutions we deliver.
